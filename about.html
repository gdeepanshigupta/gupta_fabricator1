About

A fabrication workshop focused on honest advice and reliable metalwork.

Gupta Fabricator works with homeowners, shop owners, contractors, and small industries who need durable iron and steel work with professional finishing.

Our approach

We make fabrication easier to understand before the work begins.

Good metalwork depends on more than welding. We help customers choose the right section size, design pattern, finish, locking system, hinge quality, and installation method before production starts.

That clarity reduces rework and gives the customer a finished product that fits the site properly.

Strength

We recommend material and sections based on real usage, span, weight, and safety.

Finish

We pay attention to alignment, weld cleaning, edges, primer, paint, polish, and coating.

Service

We support repairs, modification, and maintenance after installation when needed.
